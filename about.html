Welcome to Soufriere Kiddies Cricket Club.

Here at Soufriere Kiddies Cricket Club we proudly take part in the nurturing of young children from soufriere and surrounding areas natural abilities. We determine their aptitude for cricket and guide them to realize their potential at an early age. Head coach Mr. Darren Sammy pecializes in identifying strong and weak points and improve them in all areas while also determining their most suitable role on the pitch alongside club's manager Mr Maxwell Johnny.
